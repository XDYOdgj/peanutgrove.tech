Speidel Scrub Petite Watch – Stylish & Functional for Healthcare Professionals

The Speidel Scrub Petite Watch is a must-have accessory for medical professionals and nursing students. Designed with both form and function in mind, it's built to keep up with the demands of a healthcare career. The standout red second hand makes it easy to track pulses, ensuring quick and accurate monitoring during busy shifts.

Equipped with a 3-hand Japanese Quartz Movement, this watch offers precise timekeeping that you can rely on. The luminous hands are perfect for visibility in low light settings, adding comfort and reliability to your daily routine. Its compact 28mm size fits seamlessly on a wide range of wrist sizes, from 5 inches to 7.8 inches, making it a versatile fit for everyone.

The ultra-durable silicone band is not only easy to clean but also water-resistant up to 50M, making it ideal for use in clinical environments or during active days. Whether you're in a hospital, clinic, or nursing school, this watch is designed to be worn comfortably throughout the day.

With a variety of silicone band colors available, it's easy to match your watch to your scrubs for a cohesive, professional look. This nurse watch is more than just a timepiece—it's a practical addition to your nursing school supplies or medical uniform, simplifying your routine while helping you stay on time.
